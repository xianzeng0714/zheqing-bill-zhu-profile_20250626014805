About Me

Zheqing (Bill) Zhu is a highly accomplished researcher and engineer at the forefront of artificial intelligence, machine learning, and neuroscience. His passion lies in translating complex theoretical research into tangible, real-world applications. Currently, as Founder and CEO of Pokee AI, he is pioneering the next generation of AI agents. His journey is one of relentless pursuit of knowledge and innovation, from groundbreaking work in reinforcement learning to his current endeavors in AI-driven solutions for neurodegenerative diseases.
